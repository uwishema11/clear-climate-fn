About ClearClimate

Clear Climate is an Afrocentric Non Governmental Organization (NGO) geared towards Climate Action. We are focused on educating, sensitising, and empowering individuals, communities, and organisations towards creating a safer climate. This is made possible by building a robust network of volunteers and sponsors, and connecting them to organisations and projects to help drive and achieve the goal of a healthier environment and safer climate.

Plant a tree

This project encourages the process of reforestation and afforestation which simply means the planting of trees in specific areas. Trees naturally have the ability to capture and trap in carbon dioxide from the environment. By so doing, we’re able to prevent harmful carbon from going straight to the atmosphere which poses as a threat and can lead to climate crisis in the long run.

Turn off your lights

Greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, etc are actually important to help keep the earth warm and stable. However, most of our fluorescent bulbs emit high amount of these gases which then goes beyond keeping the earth warm, to actually causing global warming and a threat to life on earth. It is therefore encouraged to switch from high voltage fluorescent bulbs to low watt led lights. We also encourage that you turn off lights that are not in use, and opt in for natural lighting in most cases.

Get yourself a solar system

Many countries in the world rely on generators as a source of energy. As much as they provide you with electricity, they also emit dangerous carbon that is detrimental to your health and to the atmosphere at large. Rather than causing danger to the climate by increasing the risk of global warming, we encourage communities and individuals to opt for solar systems as an alternative to generators.
